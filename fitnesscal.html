Our Fitness Calculator

Using a fitness calculator is the perfect way to make every part of your fitness journey more systematic and successful. Here are just a few ways they can help you optimize your training and nutrition toward your goals.

1. Use a one-rep max (1RM) calculator to choose weights that line up with your goals: building muscle, losing fat, or gaining strength or explosive power. And you don't even need to know your 1RM to use it!

2. Calculate your maximum heart rate (MHR) to tailor your cardio to specific goals and difficultly levels.

3. Determine your ideal daily calories or macros to make more strategic food choices to meet your health, muscle-gain, or weight-loss goals.
